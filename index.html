About Our Instruction

The TechPromise program is a model of collaboration at it's finest.
With multiple teams, organizations, and corporations coming together, students were exposed to:

  • Intro to Programming using Scratch
  • Drone Programming and Piloting
  • 3D Printing
  • Cyber Security
  • Web Design & Front-End Web Development
  • Game Development
  • Tech Career Exploration
  • Stock Market Investing
  • S.M.A.R.T. Goal Setting
  • Resume Writing & Interview Skills
  • Networking and more!

Thank you to all of the partners and volunteers who helped bring tech exposure to AtPromise students! We can not do this without you!
